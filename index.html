About Us

Founded in Vancouver, one of the most vibrant and cultural cities in Canadá, HypeModel has a history that dates back to 1990. Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring beauty to life uniqueness and the extraordinary potential of talented individuals. With years of experience in the fashion, we have built a dedicated and passionate team ready to launch new talent and support models already established in their careers.
